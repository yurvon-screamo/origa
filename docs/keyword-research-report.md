### Localization / Cultural Notes (English Market)

- **"Learn japanese" vs "study japanese":** "learn" dominates by 5-10x in search volume. Always use "learn".
- **Reddit is a major discovery channel:** "reddit" appears as a subtype modifier across nearly every app-related query. Engaging r/LearnJapanese, r/japanese, r/kanji communities is critical for pre-launch.
- **"Best" + "reddit" combo queries** are very common — blog posts comparing apps with Reddit-sourced opinions rank well.
- **Privacy/local AI is NOT a search demand** (no autocomplete suggestions) but is a differentiator for the "privacy-conscious" persona. Should be used in content marketing, not as primary keyword.
- **"Offline" is a meaningful modifier** — it appears in Suggest for "learn japanese offline" with subtypes for apps, APK, PC, free. This maps directly to Origa's architecture.
- **Anime/manga as learning content** has a dedicated following — "learn japanese from manga", "japanese learning app with anime" are real queries.

### Localization / Cultural Notes (Russian Market)

- **"С нуля" (from zero) is a dominant modifier** — appears in nearly every Japanese learning query. Always include "с нуля" in content targeting beginners.
- **"Самостоятельно" (independently/self-study) is important** — many Russian learners study without tutors. The combination "изучение японского языка с нуля самостоятельно" is a real query with zero competition.
- **Yandex is significant** — while Google.ru handles many queries, Yandex Wordstat shows 2-5x higher volumes for Japanese learning terms. Yandex SEO (metadata, structured data) matters.
- **Geographic modifiers are common** — "екатеринбург", "владивосток", "хабаровск", "в москве" appear in Suggest. These signal active demand in Russian Far East cities near Japan.
- **"Кандзи это" (what is kanji)** suggests many beginners don't even know what kanji is — educational content at the top of funnel.
- **"Хирагана катакана тренажер" (trainer)** — tool-intent queries. A free Hiragana/Katakana trainer web page could drive significant traffic.
- **Anki has strong adoption in Russia** — "anki японский", "колоды" are active queries. Origa can position as a modern, integrated Anki alternative.
- **"На русском" (in Russian) is a unique RU-market signal** — people specifically search for Japanese learning resources in Russian. Origa's native Russian UI directly addresses this underserved need.
